# S01E01 Dialogue - "The Map That Forgot Roads"

## Rule of the Day
**"Name the path. Promise to keep it."**

---

## Scene Breakdown

### ACT 1: SETUP (S01-S04)

**S02 - The Brothers on the Ridge**
- **Charlie:** "Fold says the crossroads should be just ahead."

**S03 - Fold's Trouble**
- **Fold:** "The lines... they're fading. I can't remember where the road goes."

**S04 - Spencer's Impulse**
- **Spencer:** "Shortcut! I'll find one!"

---

### ACT 2: RISING ACTION (S05-S09)

**S06 - Spencer Lost**
- **Spencer:** "Wait... I was just here. The grass moved!"

**S07 - Charlie Investigates**
- **Charlie:** "The old path markers are here, but they're blank."

**S08 - Fletcher's Insight**
- **Fletcher:** "Someone named these paths once. Names have power."

**S09 - Fold's Sadness**
- **Fold:** "When names are forgotten, I forget too."

---

### ACT 3: CLIMAX (S10-S13)

**S10 - Charlie's Plan**
- **Charlie:** "We need to name the path ourselves. And promise to remember it."

**S11 - Fletcher Names the Gentle Way**
- **Fletcher:** "The Gentle Way. I name you, and I promise to keep you."

**S12 - Spencer Names the Quick Run**
- **Spencer:** "The Quick Run! I'll remember you!"

**S13 - Charlie Names the Steady Road**
- **Charlie:** "The Steady Road. I give you my word."

---

### ACT 4: RESOLUTION (S14-S17)

**S14 - Fold Restored**
- **Fold:** "I remember! Three paths, three promises!"

**S16 - Walking the Path**
- **Fletcher:** "Names and promises. They shape the world."

---
